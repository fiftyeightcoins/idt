What is Inventory Management?
Core Definition
1

Process of tracking goods, managing stock levels, controlling storage and movement of inventory throughout supply chain operations.

Business Impact
5

Foundation for operational efficiency and customer satisfaction, directly influencing profitability and competitive advantage.

Key Objective
2

Ensure right product availability at right time, balancing customer demand with operational efficiency requirements.

Essential Functions
4

Real-time tracking, stock level optimization, and movement control enable data-driven decision making.

3
Primary Goal

Avoid shortage and excess stock simultaneously, maintaining optimal inventory levels for sustainable business operations.
